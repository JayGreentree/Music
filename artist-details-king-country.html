for King & Country

Growing up in a musical family, for KING & COUNTRY's Joel and Luke Smallbone have been singing and performing most of their lives.The brothers moved to America in the early 90’s when their father took a job in Nashville, Tennessee.

After traveling the world, and performing in live shows, Joel and Luke decided to focus on making music together, drawing from their lives:“Music has the peculiar ability to connect with the soul, and we want to use it to encourage... Life is a journey; full of highs and lows; triumphs and sorrows; these are the subjects we write about: Real human experiences.”
